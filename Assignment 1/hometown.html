City of São Paulo

São Paulo Buildings. Source: pixabay

Information

  • State: São Paulo
  • Population: 12,400,232
  • Mayor: Ricardo Nunes

São Paulo is located in the Southeast region of Brazil. It is the most populous city in Brazil and in the Americas. The city is known for its multiculturalism as it was built by waves of immigration from Portuguese, Japanese, Italians, and Arabs.

*Trivia: São Paulo has the largest Japanese and Italian community outside Japan and Italy. Also there are more Lebanese descendents in Brazil than the population of Lebanon.
